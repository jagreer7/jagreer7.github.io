Graphic Design and 3D Modeling


"Form" is a class I took in the Fall of 2022. This class taught 3D Modelling and fabrication through Autodesk's Fusion 360 and Ultimaker's CURA. In this class, I learned modeling and fabrication techniques and applied them to solve exciting challenges.

"Image" is a class I took in the Spring of 2022. This class is an introduction to the world of images. However, this class wasn't just about snapping photos. This class covered photography, photo editing, Photoshop, basic animation, and even virtual reality. We even covered some of the history and deeper side of photography and images. The projects and challenges this class presented me were "out of this world" fun and I am so excited to share them with you!
