About Me

This is a larger scale, end to end, design project. The task, design something to enhance those times when you’re alone. Waiting for the bus, hanging before class, sitting in line.


The project starts with the first step, Observation. I needed to take note of the times when I am alone. What do I do? What Habits? What activities? Using this information, along with those of my group, we could create a potential idea of how we could enhance this time. The observation is here:


Download My Observations as .pdf


The second step is breaking down these observations into two key parts. The POV map and the empathy map.


The empathy map takes the observations and breaks them into parts like “What are the activities we do while were alone?”, and “Why do we do these things?”.
