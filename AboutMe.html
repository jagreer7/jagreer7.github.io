Who am I?

I guess I could be labelled as a lot of things. I’m a male, Asian, 5’10”, walks on two feet, etc…

Of course I’m more than those things on the outside. I’m a brother, a student, a learner, a friend to all, a Pokémon hunter, an Eagle Scout, a video game collector, a wannabe designer and a succulent caregiver. I spend my free time doing homework and playing video games. I love food and the history and science which surrounds it. In a lot of ways I am confident I am just like you.
