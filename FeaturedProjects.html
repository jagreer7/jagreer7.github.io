Enter the Honktagon - A Text Based RPG


Enter the Honktagon is a single players, text based, role playing adventure that has the user fighting against enemy geese, navagating a map, and colling and purchasing items. Coded in C++ and built to be played in the users terminal, Enter the Honktagon is a demonstration of understanding including two dimensional arrays, data structures, and coding as a team.

Co-Designer: Jackson Greer

Co-Designer: Ryan Fuentez - ryfu3617@colorado.edu

Get Lost - The Collaborative Maze Game


Get Lost - The Collaborative Maze Game is a game that requires 2 players, the driver and the navigator, who must work together to navagate a maze. This game utilizes Arduino and P5.js to map arcade style buttons and a custom built 64 LED grid to generate mazes and player positions.

Co-Designer: Jackson Greer

Co-Designer: James Tran - James.Tran-1@colorado.edu

Co-Designer: Max Epperson - maep5290@colorado.edu

Blood in the Roots - A 2 Player Board Game


Blood in the Roots is a competitive 2 player board game that has rival wizards facing off to win tree growing cometitions. This game is a demonstration of the full game design process as well as fabrication both digitally and physically.

Co-Designer: Jackson Greer

Trace and Spell


Trace and Spell is a tabletop device that allows children to place a paper and trace letters to spell simple words. by spelling proper words, children get to see their word come to life on the laptop screen. This is a display of a neopixel grid
