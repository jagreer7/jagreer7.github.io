Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

The previous challenges present many accessibility issues and many opportunities to improve. First, since challenge 2 is in a table format, a visually impaired user, and their devices, would struggle to determine what is on the screen. A solution would be to use photos and more advanced code so that alt text could be utilized. Challenge 3 and 4 are fairly accessible because they are very text-based. A text reader could navigate these formats with relative ease. Challenges which require user input may be difficult for those who cannot perform these tasks. A solution would be the implementation of voice controls to give the interfaces more usability.
